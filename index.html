Two Detroit retirees. One beach. A whole lot of Coney sauce.

Scott and Trina Legas spent years running concessions back home in Detroit. After retiring, they started doing what so many Michiganders do — wintering on Fort Myers Beach. The dream of opening a little coney island spot of their own followed them south, and Detroit Dawgs is what happened when they finally said yes to it.

"We started out for fun. We weren't sure we were going to do it full-time because it's a lot of work." — Trina

What began as a side project is now a fixture on Estero Blvd — a real Detroit-and-Flint flavor, served by real Detroit people, on a beach full of fellow snowbirds who know exactly what a proper Coney dog should taste like.
