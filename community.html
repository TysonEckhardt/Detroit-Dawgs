Community

We're part of this island, not just parked on it

🎆

Friday Fireworks Fundraiser

Every Friday, we donate a portion of our sales to local businesses raising money for Fort Myers Beach's Fourth of July fireworks show. Stop in on a Friday and your Coney dog does double duty.

🎟️

Island Charm Crawl

We're proud participants in the Island Charm Crawl — a summer event where folks grab a $30 gift bag and collect charms from local businesses across the island. It's a fun way to explore Fort Myers Beach while supporting the small businesses that make it special, us included.

🚗

Local Car Shows

You'll find us showing up at local car show events too — because a Coney dog and a good car show just go together, and it's another chance to meet our neighbors and our regulars face to face.

An island business, through and through

Scott and Trina built Detroit Dawgs as part of this community, not just a business inside it. If you're visiting and want to know what's happening locally, ask us at the window — we probably know, and we're probably going.
